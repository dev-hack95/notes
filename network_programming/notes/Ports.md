# `Ports`

  * Port numbers are 16 bit non-negative integers(range 0-65535). These numbers are abstract
    and do not refer to anything physical.

  * Standard port numbrs are assigned by the internet assigned numbers authority (IANA).
    The set of numbers is dividded into special ranges, includding the well-known port
    numbers (0-1023), the registered port numbers (1024-49151) and dynamic/private port
    numbers (49152-65535). Traditionallly, servers wishing to bindd to a well-known port
    require apeecial lpprivalges such as administrator ot "root" access
